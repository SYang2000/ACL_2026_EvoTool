Why is the parent so often init? Diversity-aware selection retains every policy that wins at least one selection instance — including the initial policy — and parents are sampled proportional to instance wins, so init keeps being re-selected as long as it still wins instances. Deep chains form when an evolved policy overtakes it (e.g. the deployed g48-planner lineage).
